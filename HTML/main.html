Inappropriate content: content (usually multimedia) that is not suitable for all ages and maturity levels. We are often led to it by mistake. Addiction: internet addiction exists and many of our fellow human beings suffer in silence. Unsolicited messages: something that may well lead to bullying, messages from strangers can either carry a virus to our computer or give away our location. Alienation, psychological impact of overuse (psychological impact of addiction): in connection with the preceding risk, the influence of bullying can be strongly discouraging. Otherwise, there is always the potential for depression or other mental health damage.
